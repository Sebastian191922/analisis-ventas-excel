# Sales Performance Dashboard | Superstore 2020-2023

## 📊 Project Overview
Analysis of sales data from a US retail company with 9,994 orders 
across 3 product categories and 4 regions.

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- Which region is the most profitable?
- How have sales evolved quarter by quarter?
- Which customer segment is most valuable?
- What are the top 10 best-selling products?

## 📈 Key Findings
- **Technology** is the highest revenue category at $144,000+
- **West** region leads in total sales with $700,000+
- Sales show a clear **upward trend** from Q1 to Q4
- **Consumer** segment represents the largest share of sales

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query (data cleaning)
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
